Scholarships for Students Excelling in English

National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS)

Eligibility: For students from Class 8 onwards. Minimum 55% marks in previous year’s exams (50% for SC/ST). Family income should not exceed ₹1.5 lakh per annum.

Award: ₹12,000 per annum.

Application Process: Apply through the National Scholarship Portal (NSP).

Vidyagama Scholarship for Merit Students

Eligibility: For students in SSLC (10th) and PUC. Must score 85% or above in board exams.

Award: Incentives range from ₹10,000 to ₹20,000.

Application Process: Apply through the vidyadhan Scholarship Portal (NSP).

Karnataka State Merit-Cum-Means Scholarship

Eligibility: For students from Class 10 onwards. Must have scored at least 70% in the previous year.

Award: Covers tuition fees and provides maintenance support.

Application Process: Apply online through Karnataka Scholarship Portal (SSP).

Incentive for SSLC & PUC Top Scorers

Eligibility: Top-performing students in SSLC (Class 10) and PUC (Class 12) board exams. Typically awarded to the top 1,000 scorers in the state.

Award: ₹10,000 for SSLC and ₹20,000 for PUC.

Application Process: Apply through the Incentive for SSLC & PUC Top Scorers.

Pratibha Puraskar Scholarship

Eligibility: For SSLC and PUC students who have scored 90% or above in board exams. Family income should be below ₹2.5 lakh per annum.

Award: Financial aid to help with further studies.

Application Process: Apply through the Pratibha Puraskar Scholarship.

Inspire Scholarship (Department of Science & Technology)

Eligibility: PUC students excelling in science subjects (top 1% of their class). Must pursue further education in science streams.

Award: ₹80,000 per annum.

Application Process: Apply through the Inspire Scholarship portal.
